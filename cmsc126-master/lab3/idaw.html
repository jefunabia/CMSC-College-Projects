Idaw

Idaw

Idaw is a war dance from Luzon, specifically in the northern part of Luzon (Cordillera region). The dance also represents a war ceremony. Idaw is a kind of bird that a certain tribal group wants to capture since the tribal group believes that the bird is a lucky charm during wars. The bird could give them a higher chance of winning in their war.
