Buchaechum

"Buchaechum is taken from Korean, Buchae which means Fan and Chum which means dance.The traditional dance of Buchaechum itself originally was derived from ancient rituals thousands of years ago which was displayed at a worship ceremony to the gods of Korean Shamanism. Korean shamanism itself is an ancient belief of Korean society that also combines beliefs and is influenced by native religions such as Buddhism and Taoism."
